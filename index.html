COME WITH ME

As I explore Pasadena.

Spending time in Pasadena for over 10 years now, I have never really got to truly explore this city until I turned 21. This city has a lot of offer. This city has been growing more and more within the years, with new restaurants, new cafes, and many events. I began to love this city when I had my first job in old town pasadena. Working here for 5 years, I have seen many different people come contribute to many of pasadena's events such as farmers markets, chalk festivals, movie showings, and many more. So follow me as I venture through pasadena with my friends and loved ones.
