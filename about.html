ABOUT



Hello & Welcome !

My name is Phoebe J Lee and this is the website that let's you venture through Pasadena with me and my friends. I don't do this alone... I often have the help and company of my friends, like Robbie, Jihoo, and others. Hopefully you will hear about them in my blogs. Most of the time my partner in crime will be Robbie, as he has helped my photograph and give input on some information. This website will show you the best places to study, new and great places to eat, places to let loose, and places you can just simply enjoy. With a lot of experience of what goes on in Pasadena, I want to show you what a beautiful, fun, and community-friendly this place is! Through my experiences and plans, I hope I help you learn more about Pasadena, as well as encourage you to visit!
